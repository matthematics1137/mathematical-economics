Mathematical Economics

Interactive notes and programs inspired by Kelvin (Kevin) Lancaster’s Mathematical Economics.

This site collects concise, visual explanations and small interactive models across consumer theory, production, equilibrium, games, growth, and more. Each page aims to make the math tangible with clean, dependency‑free demos you can tweak in the browser.
